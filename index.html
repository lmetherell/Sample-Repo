LOVE LIVING IN NORTHERN NEVADA

I love living in Northern Nevada for the many outdoor activities it offers, the great weather, wonderful people, and the landscape diversity from Lake Tahoe to the desert. As an added bonus we have no state income tax in Nevada. It is possible to ski and golf on the same day. It is a great place to live.
